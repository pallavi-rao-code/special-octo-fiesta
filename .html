Shutter Speed

If you don't know what the shutter it's a device located on the inside of the camera which covers the image sensor
The shutter speed is the speed at which that device moves.
You use a higher shutter speed for things that move faster or are in motion and a lower shutter speed for stationary. They also affect if you have an over or underexposed photo but you can fix that with exposure. Shutter Speed
